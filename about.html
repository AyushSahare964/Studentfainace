About This Project

This Finance Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to empower users with better control over their personal finances. It enables you to set daily and monthly spending limits, track expenses by category, visualize spending patterns, monitor savings goals, and manage miscellaneous outlays. Our goal is to provide a simple yet powerful tool to help you achieve financial wellness.
